I spent four days in Brussels. Very nice city, and I was lucky to have good weather the whole time. I wandered around the streets taking pictures.

![Crosswalk lines echoing the facade of a modern building](/blog/brussels-trip/crosswalk.jpg)

A crosswalk I came back to multiple times because I thought this shot could be interesting. I'm not fully convinced with the result, but I like the geometry of the vertical lines on the building echoing the crosswalk stripes.

![A kid running across a rooftop terrace overlooking the city](/blog/brussels-trip/kid-running.jpg)

I saw this kid running and tried to capture the shot right when he was crossing that shadow line, to get this bright/dark contrast.

![A man reading behind a cafe window](/blog/brussels-trip/man-cafe.jpg)

This man at the cafe caught my eye. I like that he's looking right at me, and the light is quite nice too.

![A kid on his smartphone, sitting next to a vintage photograph of people reading newspapers](/blog/brussels-trip/smartphone.jpg)

A kid on his smartphone. What's ironic is the picture right next to him, with people reading newspapers.
